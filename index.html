Welcome to Central PA Estate & Elder Law.

Welcome to Central PA Estate & Elder Law PC. With our primary office in the Greenfield Corporate Center, Lancaster, PA and a satellite office in the Rossmoyne Business Park, Mechanicsburg, PA, we serve seniors and their families across Central Pennsylvania and primarily in Lancaster, Dauphin and Cumberland counties. We have built our practice on client-centered principals and “old-fashioned” but not out dated, Central Pennsylvania values. We have adopted the covered bridge as our symbol, to illustrate these time honoured values and to show that we will shelter and protect you and your loved ones across the often challenging and troubled waters of aging.

See what we can do for you and your loved ones.

Giving seniors and their families’ effective guidance, education and the proper legal tools, through the implementation of a proven and coordinated senior care plan is what makes Central PA Estate & Elder Law different and most effective. Whether pre-planning to avoid a crisis, or struggling in the face of an imminent -health related crisis, we can develop and implement a senior care plan that will get you the care you need, identify sources to help pay for your care and save your home and as much of your assets as the law allows for you, your spouse, and your loved ones. Call us today at 1-800-497-4103 to schedule an appointment with an experienced elder law attorney who can show you how Central PA Estate & Elder Law can help you meet the legal, financial and health care challenges you may be facing.
